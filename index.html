Snow Control

Winter is coming. The recent NOAA forcast predicts increased average temperatures with near average precipitation for this coming winter. What this means for the south shore is ~40" of snowfall with greater chance for melting and slush during the day. Don't let that slush become ice! K Agra offers residential and small commercial snow removal, along with options for pet safe deicers and emergency service. Please reach out for more details.
